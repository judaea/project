[COLOR dodgerblue][B]Add/Remove Function[/B][/COLOR]
[I]In David there is an Add/Remove context menu item for Movies and TV Shows. This is where you can add/remove a Movie or TV Show to David Favorites, Subscriptions or a Trakt list. The remove option also has 'Remove from cache', which will refresh all metadata for the chosen Movie or TV Show. Try it out to see...[/I]
